Human Trafficking – Modern Slavery & Global Challenges

🌍 Bài đọc:

Human trafficking stands as a pervasive and pernicious contemporary scourge, a flagrant affront to fundamental human dignity and rights. Far from being a relic of the past, this clandestine industry has metastasized into a multi-billion-dollar global enterprise, rivaling the drug and arms trade in its scope. It constitutes the acquisition of people by improper means such as force, fraud, or coercion, for the purpose of exploitation. The victims, often the most vulnerable segments of society—those fleeing conflict, poverty, or systemic discrimination—are entrapped in a cycle of servitude. The sheer ubiquity of the phenomenon, spanning from forced labor in sweatshops and agricultural sectors to sexual exploitation and forced criminality, underscores the complexity and the chilling pervasiveness of this modern-day slavery.

The mechanisms employed by traffickers are disturbingly sophisticated and often prey on the victim’s desperation or naivety. Grooming and psychological manipulation are frequently deployed before physical force, creating a facade of opportunity—a high-paying job, a chance at a better life—that rapidly dissolves into an untenable reality of coercion. Once entrapped, victims face rigorous control methods. These can include debt bondage, where exorbitant fees for transportation or ‘services’ are impossible to repay, and the confiscation of identity documents, rendering the individual undocumented and fearful of seeking external aid.

The aftermath of trafficking is not merely physical; it leaves indelible psychological stigmata. Victims frequently suffer from complex PTSD, chronic health issues, and profound social isolation, making reintegration into mainstream society a daunting prospect. From a legal and judicial standpoint, prosecuting these cases presents a unique set of impediments. The cross-border nature of the crime, the difficulty in obtaining unimpeachable victim testimony (often compromised by trauma or fear of reprisal), and the corruption that can permeate enforcement agencies collectively contribute to an alarmingly low conviction rate.

Therefore, a cohesive and resolute global response is imperative to dismantle this illicit network. International protocols, such as the UN’s Palermo Protocol, provide a crucial framework for cooperation, but their effective implementation remains variable. This inconsistency highlights the need for future strategies that focus on addressing the root causes, such as economic disparity, lack of educational opportunities, and political instability. Furthermore, technological innovation, including the use of blockchain to verify supply chains and the deployment of AI to track online recruitment, offers nascent but promising avenues for disrupting the traffickers’ operational fluidity.

(Adapted from The Guardian)

❓ Câu hỏi (6–13):

6
Which of the following is NOT mentioned in the passage regarding the challenges faced by victims of trafficking?
Aprofound social isolation
Bchronic health problems
Cprolonged joblessness
Dcomplex PTSD

✓ Đáp án đúng: C. prolonged joblessness

Giải thích chi tiết: Căn cứ vào đoạn 3: "Victims frequently suffer from complex PTSD, chronic health issues, and profound social isolation" → A, B, D đều được đề cập trực tiếp. "Prolonged joblessness" (thất nghiệp kéo dài) không hề xuất hiện. Nạn nhân bị ép lao động cưỡng bức, chứ không phải "không có việc làm".

Phân tích từng đáp án:
A (Sai – được đề cập): "profound social isolation" có trong đoạn 3.
B (Sai – được đề cập): "chronic health issues" có trong đoạn 3.
C (Đúng – không được đề cập): Không có thông tin về thất nghiệp kéo dài.
D (Sai – được đề cập): "complex PTSD" xuất hiện rõ trong đoạn 3.

7
The word exorbitant in paragraph 2 is OPPOSITE in meaning to ______.
Areasonable
Boutrageous
Cextortionate
Dastronomical

✓ Đáp án đúng: A. reasonable

Giải thích: "Exorbitant" /ɪɡˈzɔː.bɪ.tənt/ = quá đắt, cắt cổ, phi lý. Ngữ cảnh: "exorbitant fees for transportation ... impossible to repay" → phí quá cao không thể trả nổi. Trái nghĩa là "reasonable" (hợp lý, phải chăng).

Phân tích từng đáp án:
A. reasonable (hợp lý) → trái nghĩa ĐÚNG.
B. outrageous (quá quắt) → đồng nghĩa, không phải trái nghĩa.
C. extortionate (cắt cổ) → đồng nghĩa hoàn toàn.
D. astronomical (lớn như thiên văn) → nghĩa bóng chỉ mức rất cao, cũng đồng nghĩa.

8
Which of the following best paraphrases the underlined sentence in paragraph 1?
"Far from being a relic of the past, this clandestine industry has metastasized into a multi-billion-dollar global enterprise, rivaling the drug and arms trade in its scope."
AFar from declining, this covert trade has ballooned into a global enterprise worth millions, standing on the same scale as international drug and weapons trafficking.
BContrary to the belief that it belongs to the past, this secretive industry has exploded in size, becoming a multi-billion-dollar global network linked with the drug and arms markets.
CInstead of diminishing over time, this underground sector has expanded dramatically, evolving into a global business worth billions and matching the scale of drug and arms trafficking.
DFar from being outdated, this legal industry has grown into a worldwide enterprise generating billions, placing it on par with the global drug and weapons trades.

✓ Đáp án đúng: C

Giải thích chi tiết: Câu gốc gồm các ý: (1) không phải di tích quá khứ → không suy giảm; (2) "metastasized" (di căn) → mở rộng mạnh mẽ; (3) multi-billion-dollar global enterprise; (4) rivaling the drug and arms trade (ngang tầm với buôn ma túy và vũ khí).

Phân tích đáp án:
A: Sai vì "millions" (hàng triệu) khác với "billions" (hàng tỷ).
B: Sai vì "linked with" (liên kết với) không chính xác bằng "rivaling" (ngang tầm/cạnh tranh).
C: Đúng – giữ nguyên các ý: thay vì suy giảm → mở rộng mạnh, trị giá hàng tỷ, ngang tầm buôn ma túy và vũ khí.
D: Sai vì "legal industry" (ngành hợp pháp) – trong khi bài nói "clandestine" (bí mật, bất hợp pháp).

Which of the following is TRUE according to the passage?

Phân tích từng đáp án:
A: Sai – đoạn 1 khẳng định buôn người là "multi-billion-dollar global enterprise", không phải vấn đề nhỏ hay suy giảm.

D: Sai – đoạn 4: blockchain "offers nascent but promising avenues" (còn sơ khai, mới hứa hẹn), không phải "chắc chắn xóa bỏ".

The phrase “prey on” in paragraph 2 mostly means ______.
Aexploit a weakness
Bgive comfort to

✓ Đáp án đúng: A. exploit a weakness

Giải thích: "Prey on" trong ngữ cảnh "traffickers prey on the victim's desperation or naivety" có nghĩa là lợi dụng điểm yếu (sự tuyệt vọng hoặc cả tin) để trục lợi hoặc gây hại. Đây là nghĩa bóng của "săn mồi".

Phân tích:
A. exploit a weakness (lợi dụng điểm yếu) → Đúng.

C. share belongings with (chia sẻ đồ đạc) → Sai.
D. protect from danger (bảo vệ khỏi nguy hiểm) → Sai, trái nghĩa.
